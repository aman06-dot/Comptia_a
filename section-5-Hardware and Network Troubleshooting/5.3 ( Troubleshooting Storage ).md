## Troubleshooting Storage Devices

### Storage failure symptoms

- Read/Write failure
	- "Cannot read from the source disk"
- Slow performance
	- Constant LED activity
	- Retry...retry...retry
- Loud clicking noise
	- The click of death
	- May also include grinding and scraping
