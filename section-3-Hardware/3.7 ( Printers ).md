## Laser Printers

### Laser printer
- Combine a laser, high voltage, charged ions, powered ink, heat and paper
- Very high quality
- Fast printing speeds
- Very complex
	- Many complex 
	- Requires on-printer memory
	- Messy on the inside

### Imaging drum
- Image is drawn onto a photosensitive drum
	- "Painted" with a laser
